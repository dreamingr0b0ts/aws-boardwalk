Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026

Information we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect the information you provide: name, email address, company, phone number, service interest, and your message. Our infrastructure also records standard technical logs (such as IP address and request metadata) for security and abuse prevention.

How we use it

Contact-form submissions are delivered by email to our team and used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing. Technical logs are used only to operate and secure the site.

Cookies and analytics

This site does not set cookies and does not run third-party advertising or tracking scripts.

Data retention

Inquiry emails are retained as ordinary business correspondence. Rate-limiting records tied to form submissions expire automatically within days. You may request deletion of your inquiry at any time by emailing us.

Third-party services

The site is hosted on Amazon Web Services; form submissions are transmitted via AWS services (API Gateway, Lambda, and Simple Email Service) to our mailbox. AWS processes this data as our service provider.

Your rights

To access, correct, or delete information you have sent us, contact info@planetek.org.
