# Short-Term Rental Program

## Overview

Short-term rentals (STRs) — dwellings rented for fewer than 30 consecutive days — require an
STR license in addition to a business license. Alpenglow caps non-owner-occupied STR licenses
at 250 citywide; owner-occupied licenses are not capped.

## License types and fees

Owner-occupied (primary residence) STR licenses cost $200 per year. Non-owner-occupied
licenses cost $500 per year and are subject to the citywide cap and a waitlist. Both types
require a life-safety self-certification and proof of at least $500,000 in liability coverage.

## Operating requirements

Every STR must post the license number in all advertising, designate a local contact who can
respond within 60 minutes, provide off-street parking as shown on the license, and limit
occupancy to 2 persons per bedroom plus 2. Quiet hours are 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM. Trash must be
in wildlife-resistant containers.

## Lodging tax

STR operators collect and remit the city lodging tax of 5.5 percent monthly through the
online tax portal, in addition to state and county taxes. Platforms that collect on the
operator's behalf must be listed on the license.

## Violations

Three verified complaints within 12 months, or operating without a license, triggers a
revocation hearing. Operating unlicensed carries a fine of up to $1,000 per day and a
one-year ineligibility period.
