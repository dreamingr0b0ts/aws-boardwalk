# Building Permits

## When a permit is required

A building permit is required in the City of Alpenglow for any structural work, additions,
new construction, decks over 30 inches above grade, garages, carports, and finished basements.
A permit is also required for re-roofing, window replacement that changes the opening size,
and any electrical, plumbing, or mechanical alteration beyond simple fixture swaps.

## When a permit is NOT required

The following work is exempt from permitting: painting, carpeting and flooring, cabinet and
countertop replacement, fences under 6 feet, sheds under 120 square feet with no utilities,
playground equipment, and sidewalk or driveway repairs of less than 50 square feet.
Like-for-like replacement of a water heater does still require a plumbing permit because it
involves gas or electrical connections and temperature-pressure relief piping.

## Residential deck permits

A residential deck permit costs $145 and covers plan review plus two inspections (footing and
final). Decks attached to the home or more than 30 inches above grade always require a permit.
Submit a site plan showing setbacks, a framing plan, and ledger attachment details. Typical
review time is 5 business days.

## How to apply

Apply online through the Alpenglow Permit Portal or in person at City Hall, 400 Summit Avenue,
Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM. Online applications require a PDF site plan and,
for structural work, stamped drawings. Incomplete applications are returned within 2 business
days with a checklist of missing items.

## Review timelines

Standard residential permits are reviewed within 10 business days. Simple over-the-counter
permits (water heaters, furnace replacement, re-roofs) are issued the same day. Commercial
projects are reviewed within 20 business days for the first cycle and 10 business days for
each re-submittal.

## Permit expiration

Permits expire 180 days after issuance if work has not started, and after any 180-day period
with no passed inspection. One 90-day extension may be granted free of charge if requested in
writing before expiration.
