# Fee Schedule (Development Services)

## Building permit fees

Building permit fees are based on project valuation: $50 minimum for projects up to $2,000,
then $50 plus $7 per additional $1,000 of valuation up to $25,000, then a published sliding
scale above that. Plan review is 65 percent of the permit fee for projects requiring review.

## Common flat fees

Water heater replacement: $60. Furnace or AC replacement: $80. Residential re-roof: $125.
Residential deck: $145. Electrical service upgrade: $95. Solar photovoltaic (residential
rooftop): $250 flat, reviewed within 5 business days under the SolarApp+ program.

## Licensing fees

Standard business license: $75 per year. Home-based business: $40. Mobile food vendor: $150.
Contractor registration: $120 per year per trade classification. Owner-occupied short-term
rental: $200. Non-owner-occupied short-term rental: $500.

## Other charges

Re-inspection fee after a second failed inspection: $65. Work started without a permit incurs
an investigation fee equal to the permit fee (i.e., the permit cost doubles). Permit extension
beyond the free 90-day extension: $50. Records research request: $25 per hour after the first
free half hour.

## Refunds

If an application is withdrawn before review begins, 100 percent of fees are refunded. After
review begins but before issuance, 50 percent. No refunds after a permit is issued or after
any inspection is performed.
