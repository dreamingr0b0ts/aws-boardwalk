# Inspections

## Scheduling an inspection

Schedule inspections online through the Permit Portal or by calling the inspection line
before 3:00 PM for next-business-day service. Same-day inspections are available for water
heaters and furnace replacements when requested before 9:00 AM. Provide the permit number,
address, and inspection type.

## Common inspection types

Footing/setback (before concrete), foundation, rough framing, rough electrical, rough
plumbing, rough mechanical, insulation, drywall, and final. Decks require only footing and
final. Re-roofs require only a final with photos of underlayment accepted in lieu of a
mid-roof inspection for asphalt shingles.

## Preparing for inspection

Work must be accessible and the approved plans on site. Rough inspections happen before
insulation or drywall covers the work. Ladders for roof access must be set and secured by the
contractor. Dogs must be secured; inspectors will not enter yards with loose animals.

## Failed inspections and re-inspection

The first re-inspection is free. A second failed inspection for the same items carries a $65
re-inspection fee that must be paid before rescheduling. Inspection results are posted to the
portal the same day, with correction notices itemized by code section.

## Certificates of occupancy

New dwellings and commercial spaces receive a Certificate of Occupancy after all finals pass
and all fees are paid. Temporary certificates valid for 60 days may be issued when only
weather-dependent exterior items (landscaping, final grading, exterior paint) remain, with a
refundable completion deposit of $1,500.
