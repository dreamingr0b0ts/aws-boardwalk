# Contractor Registration

## Who must register

All contractors performing permitted work in Alpenglow must register with the Building
Division before a permit can be issued in their name: general contractors, electrical,
plumbing, mechanical, roofing, and demolition trades. Homeowners performing work on their own
primary residence may pull their own permits without registration after passing a short
homeowner exam.

## Registration requirements

Registration costs $120 per year per trade classification and requires: proof of general
liability insurance of at least $1,000,000 per occurrence, workers' compensation coverage (or
a signed exemption affidavit for sole proprietors), and the state license for trades that
Colorado licenses (electrical and plumbing). Registrations expire December 31 and renew at
the same fee.

## Supervisor requirements

General contractor registrations must name a qualifying supervisor who has passed the ICC
General Contractor exam or holds 5 years of documented supervisory experience. A change of
supervisor must be reported within 30 days.

## Discipline

The Building Official may suspend a registration for repeated red-tag violations, working
beyond permit scope, or insurance lapse. Suspended contractors may not schedule inspections
or pull new permits until reinstated. Appeals of suspension follow the standard appeals
process through the Building Board of Appeals.
