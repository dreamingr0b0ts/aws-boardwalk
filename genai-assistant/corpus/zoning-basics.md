# Zoning Basics

## Residential districts

Alpenglow has three residential districts. R-1 (single-family): minimum lot 6,000 sq ft,
maximum height 30 feet, setbacks 20 ft front / 5 ft side / 15 ft rear. R-2 (two-family):
duplexes allowed on lots of 7,500 sq ft or more. R-3 (multi-family): up to 24 units per acre,
maximum height 42 feet with upper-story stepbacks along street frontages.

## Accessory dwelling units

One ADU is allowed per R-1 or R-2 lot, up to 800 sq ft or 50 percent of the primary dwelling's
floor area, whichever is smaller. ADUs require one additional off-street parking space unless
the lot is within a quarter mile of a transit stop. Either the ADU or the primary dwelling
must be owner-occupied. Detached ADUs follow a 5-foot rear and side setback and a 20-foot
height limit.

## Commercial districts

C-1 (neighborhood commercial) allows retail, offices, and restaurants under 5,000 sq ft.
C-2 (downtown core) has no parking minimums and allows mixed-use buildings to 55 feet.
Drive-through facilities require a conditional use permit in all commercial districts.

## Setback variances

Variance requests go to the Board of Adjustment, meeting the second Tuesday of each month.
Applications cost $300, require notice to neighbors within 300 feet, and must demonstrate a
hardship arising from the physical characteristics of the lot — economic considerations alone
do not qualify.

## Finding your zoning

Look up any parcel's zoning on the city's online zoning map, or request a written zoning
verification letter ($50, issued within 5 business days) for lenders and title companies.
